Asked Questions

How quickly will my order be processed and shipped?

We work hard to get your order ready and shipped within 1-2 business days (Monday to Friday). Processing times can vary based on the item and current demand. You’ll receive an estimated delivery date with your order confirmation.

When can I expect to receive my order?

Delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method. Once your order is on its way, you’ll be able to track it for updates. If your order seems delayed, please reach out to us.

Made a mistake on your order? No problem!

If you realize there's an error, just let us know ASAP. We’ll do our best to fix it before your order goes into production. Once we start making your item, changes might be challenging, but we’ll certainly try to help if we can.

What if I need to update my shipping address?

If you notice an address mistake, contact us right away. If your order hasn’t shipped yet, we can update it for you. If it’s already on the way, we may not be able to make changes, so please check your details carefully before placing your order.

Can I return or get a refund for a personalized item?

Since each item is custom-made just for you, we can’t accept returns or offer refunds. However, if your order arrives damaged or if there’s an issue, please reach out to us and we’ll make sure to sort it out.

Want something extra special?

We love creating unique, personalized gifts! If you have a specific idea or design in mind, drop us a message. We’re excited to collaborate with you to bring your vision to life.

How do I get in touch with you?

For any questions or issues, please use Amazon messaging system. It’s the best way to ensure we see and respond to your message promptly. If you reply to an automated email from Etsy, it might not reach us.
